Our Promise: NoLog VPN is built on a "can't log" architecture, not just "won't log" promises. We have designed our system so that logging your activity is technically impossible through Sentinel's decentralized VPN protocol.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how NoLog VPN ("we", "our", "us", or "the App"), developed and published by Bumin Kaan ALTINBAY ("Developer"), collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the NoLog VPN application available on Google Play Store.

NoLog VPN is a decentralized VPN (dVPN) application built on the Sentinel Protocol, providing secure and private internet access through community-operated VPN nodes using WireGuard encryption.

By downloading, installing, or using NoLog VPN, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms. If you do not agree with this Policy, please do not use the App.

2. Our Decentralized "Can't Log" Architecture

Unlike traditional VPN services that promise not to log, NoLog VPN leverages Sentinel's decentralized architecture where comprehensive logging is technically impossible:

  • No single entity controls the entire network ‚Äî there is no central server that processes all traffic
  • Node operators can only see encrypted traffic passing through their node ‚Äî not the content
  • No central server stores connection metadata, browsing data, or user activity
  • VPN traffic is encrypted using WireGuard (ChaCha20-Poly1305) end-to-end
  • Even if an individual node were compromised, only partial, encrypted data fragments exist

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or process the following data:

  • Your originating (real) IP address
  • Browsing history, website visits, or URLs accessed
  • DNS queries or resolutions

  • Content of your internet traffic
  • Personal information such as name, email address, or phone number
  • Location data (GPS, cell tower, or Wi-Fi based)
  • Application usage patterns or installed app inventory

5. VPN Service & Network Traffic

NoLog VPN uses the android.net.VpnService Android system API to create a secure VPN tunnel. When the VPN is active:

  • All network traffic from your device is routed through an encrypted WireGuard tunnel to a Sentinel dVPN node
  • The VPN node decrypts the outer tunnel layer and forwards your traffic to the internet ‚Äî it cannot see the content of HTTPS-encrypted traffic
  • We do not inspect, filter, modify, log, or store any traffic passing through the VPN tunnel
  • We do not inject advertisements, tracking scripts, or any content into your traffic
  • We do not redirect, reroute, or manipulate your network traffic for monetization purposes (e.g., routing ad traffic through a different country)
  • The VPN service runs as an Android Foreground Service with a persistent notification so you always know when it is active

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard and advanced security measures to protect your data:

  • VPN Tunnel Encryption: WireGuard protocol with ChaCha20-Poly1305 authenticated encryption
  • API Communications: TLS 1.2+ (preferring TLS 1.3) for all network communications
  • Local Sensitive Data: Android EncryptedSharedPreferences (AES-256-SIV + AES-256-GCM) for sensitive local storage
  • Cryptographic Keys: Android Keystore hardware-backed storage for wallet private keys
  • Transit Encryption: All data transmitted between the App and external services is encrypted in transit

While we employ strong security measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to using commercially reasonable measures to protect your data.

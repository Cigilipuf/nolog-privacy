NoLog VPN

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

Our Promise: NoLog VPN is built on a "can't log" architecture, not just "won't log" promises. We've designed our system so that logging your activity is technically impossible.

1. Introduction

NoLog VPN ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for the NoLog VPN application ("App"), built on the Sentinel decentralized VPN network.

By using NoLog VPN, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Our "Can't Log" Architecture

Unlike traditional VPNs that promise not to log, NoLog VPN uses Sentinel's decentralized architecture where comprehensive logging is technically impossible:

  • No single entity controls the entire network
  • Node operators only see encrypted traffic
  • No central server stores connection data
  • Even if one node is compromised, they only have partial, encrypted data

3. Information We DO NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Your real IP address
  • Browsing history or website visits
  • DNS queries
  • Connection timestamps with user identifiers
  • Personal information (name, email, phone)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Application usage patterns

4. Information Stored on Blockchain

The Sentinel blockchain stores only the minimum data required for fair bandwidth billing:

  • Bandwidth Used: Total bytes transferred (not content, just count)
  • Wallet Address: Pseudonymous identifier (not linked to real identity)
  • Subscription Status: Whether subscription is active
Note: Blockchain data is publicly auditable and immutable. However, it contains no personally identifiable information and cannot be linked to your real identity or browsing activity.

5. Local Device Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Settings: Your preferences (protocol, DNS, kill switch settings)
  • Wallet Keys: Encrypted using Android Keystore
  • Server Cache: List of available servers for faster connections

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App or clear app data.

6. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob (Advertising)

For free-tier users, we display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain data for advertising purposes, including:

  • Advertising ID: A resettable identifier used to serve relevant ads
  • Device information: Device model, OS version, and screen size
  • Ad interaction data: Which ads are displayed and clicked

This data is collected directly by Google and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to this data. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings. Premium subscribers do not see any advertisements, and no advertising data is collected.

Google Play Billing

If you purchase a subscription, payment is processed by Google Play. We only receive confirmation of subscription status, not your payment details.

Sentinel Network

VPN traffic is routed through Sentinel dVPN nodes operated by independent community members. Node operators cannot see the content of your encrypted traffic.

Crash Reporting

We may receive anonymous crash reports through Google Play Console to improve app stability. These reports contain no personally identifiable information.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • WireGuard encryption (ChaCha20-Poly1305) for VPN tunnel
  • TLS 1.3 for all API communications
  • Android EncryptedSharedPreferences for sensitive local data
  • Android Keystore for cryptographic key storage

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete Local Data: Uninstall the app or clear app data
  • Control Settings: Adjust privacy features in the app
  • Disconnect: Stop using the service at any time

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing for us to delete, export, or modify on our end.

9. Children's Privacy

NoLog VPN is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us.

10. dVPN Alliance

Sentinel is a founding member of the dVPN Alliance, committed to:

  • Privacy as a fundamental human right
  • Open source, verifiable code
  • Distributed logs (no single point of collection)
  • Permissionless, censorship-resistant networks

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by updating the "Last Updated" date.
